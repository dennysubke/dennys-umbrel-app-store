If you do not have a setup key, you can sign in via your NetBird account. Your container logs already show the login link, for example:

https://login.netbird.io/activate?user_code=[YOUR CODE]

  1. Right click on the app and select Troubleshoot to open the console.
  2. In the console, you will see the address with your login code.
  3. Open the link in your browser on your PC or phone.
  4. Sign in with the same NetBird account you use for the dashboard.
  5. Approve the device. After confirmation, your Umbrel client is automatically registered.
  6. Done. The container will remain connected across restarts.
